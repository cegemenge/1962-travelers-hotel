Our Story

Legacy, hospitality, and heart.

1962 Travelers Hotel was founded in honor of my beloved mother who was born in the year 1962. Our hotel blends the warmth of family legacy with the professionalism of top-class hospitality. Located in Fari Express, Ijebu Ode, our mission is to give every guest a memorable experience — whether you’re a traveler, a businessman, or a local seeking comfort and class.
